Brief self introduction

Chao Liu is particularly interested in social network and computing science. He introduced computer approaches such as machine learning into social network analysis methods to develop his own research tools and try to address some interesting research topics. In the past 2 years, he employed social network analysis to detect the best recommendation structure in crowdfunding marketing (working for doctoral dissertation); dig High-yield investment portfolio relations in stock market (trying to earn pocket money, but failed). In the future, he hopes to accomplish some works in the information system that can be well-known.
